Avengers Endgame

Stranded in space, Tony Stark communicates a dire message to Pepper Potts as his resources diminish. Simultaneously, Thor, Black Widow, Captain America, and Bruce Banner grapple with the aftermath of Thanos' catastrophic actions. Determined to reverse the devastation, the remaining Avengers devise a plan to resurrect their fallen comrades, setting the stage for an epic confrontation.

Iron Man

In "Iron Man," Tony Stark, a brilliant industrialist, forges a powerful suit to escape captivity, giving rise to his superhero alter ego. Fueled by responsibility, he combats crime and terrorism. The film's blend of action and wit establishes Tony Stark as a pivotal figure in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, marking the beginning of his heroic journey and the evolution of the franchise.

Deadpool

"Deadpool" follows Wade Wilson, a former special forces operative turned wisecracking mercenary. Diagnosed with terminal cancer, he undergoes a rogue experiment, leaving him with accelerated healing powers but a disfigured appearance. Adopting the alter ego Deadpool, he seeks revenge on the man who subjected him to the experiment. The film blends intense action with irreverent humor as Deadpool breaks the fourth wall, addressing the audience directly. His journey involves complex relationships, including a romance with Vanessa Carlysle. Full of pop culture references and meta-humor, "Deadpool" is celebrated for its unique take on the superhero genre, embracing R-rated content and providing a refreshing, unconventional narrative.

Spider Man Across The Spider Verse

After reuniting with Gwen Stacy, Brooklyn's Spider-Man is hurled into the Multiverse. There, he meets a team of Spider-People tasked with safeguarding existence. Conflict arises among the heroes regarding a new threat, forcing Miles to confront his fellow Spiders. To save his loved ones, Miles must redefine his understanding of heroism in a pivotal challenge.

Cobra Kai

"Cobra Kai" is a sequel to the "Karate Kid" saga, reigniting the rivalry between Johnny Lawrence and Daniel LaRusso. The two reopen the Cobra Kai dojo, reigniting their students' rivalry. Amidst complicated personal lives, they navigate the challenges of mentoring the next generation while confronting their own past mistakes. The series explores redemption, friendship, and legacy..

The Umbrella Academy

The Umbrella Academy is set in a universe where 43 women around the world gave birth simultaneously at noon on October 1, 1989, although none had shown any sign of pregnancy until labor began. Seven of the children are adopted by eccentric billionaire Sir Reginald Hargreeves and turned into a superhero team that he calls "The Umbrella Academy." Hargreeves gives the children numbers rather than names, but their robot-mother, Grace, later names six of them: Luther, Diego, Allison, Klaus, Ben, and Vanya.[1] Reginald puts six of the children to work fighting crime but keeps Vanya apart from her siblings' activities, claiming she demonstrates no powers of her own.
